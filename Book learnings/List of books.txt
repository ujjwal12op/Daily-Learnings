🔥 Top Self-Help Books for Personal Growth
🧠 Mindset & Psychology
"Mindset" by Carol S. Dweck
→ Fixed vs. growth mindset; how your beliefs shape your success.

"Atomic Habits" by James Clear
→ Small changes = big results. Super practical for building good habits.

"The Power of Now" by Eckhart Tolle
→ Focus on the present moment; great for calming your mind and becoming more aware.

"The Subtle Art of Not Giving a F*ck" by Mark Manson
→ Blunt but powerful take on values, meaning, and embracing life’s struggles.

"Can’t Hurt Me" by David Goggins
→ A raw and intense read on mental toughness, discipline, and resilience.

💼 Productivity & Focus
"Deep Work" by Cal Newport
→ Teaches the importance of focused work in a distracted world.

"The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People" by Stephen Covey
→ Timeless principles for success in personal and professional life.

"Essentialism" by Greg McKeown
→ The disciplined pursuit of less; learn to focus only on what truly matters.

❤️ Emotional & Social Intelligence
"Emotional Intelligence" by Daniel Goleman
→ How EQ matters more than IQ in relationships and success.

"How to Win Friends and Influence People" by Dale Carnegie
→ A classic on improving communication and building meaningful connections.

🌱 Spirituality & Purpose
"The Alchemist" by Paulo Coelho
→ A fictional tale packed with life lessons about following your dreams.

"Man’s Search for Meaning" by Viktor Frankl
→ A Holocaust survivor’s take on purpose, suffering, and human strength.
